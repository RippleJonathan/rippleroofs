### Important: Always Verify Your Specific Section
HOA rules vary not just by community name but by section and filing within a community. Always pull the actual CC&Rs for your specific lot, not just the general community rules. Your title documents or the county appraisal district can help locate your HOA documents.

Round Rock received 1.5"–2.5" hail in the April 2026 storm. In the years before, the city was hit in 2024, 2022, and 2021 with storms producing golf-ball-sized or larger hail.

The pattern matters: if you replace an asphalt roof today, it will almost certainly face severe hail within 10-15 years. The question is whether you want to go through that cycle again or end it.

**Standing seam in hail:**
- Class 4 UL 2218 impact resistance — the highest rating available
- Dents cosmetically in severe hail but essentially never fails structurally
- A dented standing seam roof is still a functioning, watertight roof
- Insurance companies treat Class 4 metal differently than asphalt — even after a hailstorm

**Asphalt shingles in hail:**
- Class 4 shingles resist moderate hail but can still be damaged in 2"+ events
- Granule loss, cracks, and bruising reduce effective life
- Full replacement often required after a major storm, starting the cycle over

## Comparing Standing Seam Installers Near Round Rock

Round Rock benefits from being in the Austin metro with access to a large contractor base — but standing seam is a specialty skill set that most general roofing companies don't have.

**What a qualified standing seam contractor provides:**
- Manufacturer certification from the panel supplier (McElroy, Sheffield, ATAS, Englert, or equivalent)
- On-site roll-forming capability or factory-fabricated panel delivery with precise length management
- Mechanical seaming tools for low-slope applications
- Documented experience with standing seam in the Austin metro market
- Written proposals specifying panel gauge, clip type (floating vs. fixed), underlayment spec, and warranty terms

**Questions to ask before signing:**
1. Are you certified by the panel manufacturer you're proposing?
2. What underlayment are you using? (High-temp synthetic or peel-and-stick — not standard felt)
3. Are the clips floating or fixed? (Floating required for Texas thermal movement)
4. What's the seam style — snap-lock or mechanically seamed? Why is that appropriate for my roof pitch?
5. What's included in your workmanship warranty and for how long?

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does standing seam handle Round Rock's soil movement?**
Round Rock's expansive clay soils cause foundation movement that affects everything in the house — including the roof deck. Standing seam's floating clip design actually helps here: panels are not rigidly fastened, so minor deck movement doesn't stress panel joints the way it would in a rigid system.

**My Round Rock HOA says no metal roofing — is there still a metal option?**
Yes. Stone-coated steel roofing (Decra, Metro Tiles) is approved by 90-95% of Texas HOAs because it looks like traditional shingles or tile. It delivers nearly identical performance to standing seam for most homeowners. See our [Stone-Coated Steel Roofing Round Rock guide](/blog/stone-coated-metal-roofing-round-rock) for full details.

**How long does standing seam installation take in Round Rock?**
Most residential projects: 3-5 days. Homes with complex rooflines, multiple dormers, or steep pitches: 7-10 days.

**What's the permit process like in Round Rock?**
The City of Round Rock requires a roofing permit for full replacements. We pull all required permits, schedule inspections, and handle the paperwork — you don't need to do anything.

**Can I get a standing seam insurance claim paid in Round Rock after hail?**
Yes, if your roof sustained damage. We document all hail impacts with photos and measurements, provide a detailed scope of work, and communicate directly with your adjuster. If you're replacing storm-damaged shingles with standing seam, you pay the upgrade cost above the insurance payout for equivalent shingle replacement.
