## Common Roofing Problems We See Around Round Rock

After years of working on homes here, we've noticed a few recurring issues tied to our local weather.

### Hail Damage

Round Rock sees its share of hail activity. Even minor hail events can bruise shingles or dent metal panels in ways that aren't obvious from the ground but can weaken a roof's ability to shed water over time. If a storm has rolled through your neighborhood recently, it's worth a look — see our [Round Rock hail damage guide](https://www.rippleroofs.com/blog/round-rock-hail-damage-guide) for what to watch for.

### Extreme Summer Heat

Our hot summers can bake asphalt shingles from above, and poor attic ventilation can make things worse from below. Together, this can shorten a shingle roof's lifespan if ventilation isn't addressed. Curious how long your roof should realistically last here? Read our [Texas roof lifespan guide](https://www.rippleroofs.com/blog/how-long-does-a-roof-last-texas).

### Wind-Driven Rain

Round Rock's spring and fall storms often bring heavy, sideways rain that can find its way into small gaps in flashing, pipe boots, or shingle seals. These leaks can go unnoticed for months before showing up as a ceiling stain. Our [DIY roof inspection checklist](https://www.rippleroofs.com/blog/diy-roof-inspection-checklist) can help you catch early warning signs between professional inspections.
