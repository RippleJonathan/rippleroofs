We have big news for homeowners in Round Rock, Austin, and all of Central Texas.

Ripple Roofing & Construction has officially achieved **CertainTeed ShingleMaster Premier** status — the highest level of certification that CertainTeed, one of North America's largest and most respected roofing manufacturers, awards to roofing contractors.

This isn't just a badge on our website. It changes what we can offer you.

## What Is CertainTeed ShingleMaster Premier?

CertainTeed's ShingleMaster program is a tiered certification system that recognizes roofing contractors who meet rigorous standards for training, installation quality, insurance, and track record. Most certified contractors hold a standard ShingleMaster designation.

**ShingleMaster Premier is the top tier** — awarded only to contractors who have demonstrated the highest level of expertise, completed advanced CertainTeed training, and maintained a consistent record of quality installations. The Premier designation is held by a small fraction of roofing contractors nationally, and very few in the Central Texas market.

Achieving it requires more than just signing up. It requires proving — to CertainTeed's satisfaction — that your crew installs their products correctly, every time.

## What It Means For Your Roof Warranty

Here's where this directly affects you as a homeowner.

Most roofing contractors can only offer you a basic manufacturer's warranty on the shingles themselves — coverage for material defects. The **workmanship** (the actual installation) is typically covered by whatever the contractor promises, usually ranging from 1 to 10 years, and it's only as good as that contractor's staying power.

As a CertainTeed ShingleMaster Premier contractor, we can now offer **three tiers of CertainTeed-backed workmanship warranty** — meaning CertainTeed itself stands behind the quality of our installation, not just the materials:

### 🏆 Lifetime Workmanship Warranty
The gold standard. Covers installation defects for the life of the roof. Available on qualifying CertainTeed roofing systems. Transferable to future homeowners — which adds real, documentable value to your home if you ever sell.

### 🥈 30-Year Workmanship Warranty
Thirty years of CertainTeed-backed installation coverage. Available on select CertainTeed product lines. Also transferable to new owners.

### 🥉 15-Year Workmanship Warranty
Fifteen years of backed coverage on standard CertainTeed systems.

The tier you qualify for depends on which CertainTeed product system you choose for your roof. When we give you a quote, we'll walk you through exactly which warranty level applies — at no extra cost to the consultation.

## Why a Manufacturer-Backed Warranty Matters

Think about what a contractor-only warranty actually means: if that company closes, merges, or the owner retires in 8 years, your warranty is worthless. It's a piece of paper with a promise from someone who may not be around to honor it.

A **CertainTeed-backed warranty** is different. CertainTeed has been manufacturing roofing products since 1904 and operates across North America. Your warranty is registered with them directly. If there's ever a workmanship-related claim, CertainTeed is part of that process.

For most homeowners, their roof is one of the largest single investments they'll make in their property. Having the manufacturer — not just the contractor — standing behind the installation is a meaningful protection.

## The Warranty Is Transferable — Here's Why That Matters If You Sell

One of the most overlooked aspects of a premium roofing warranty is what happens when you sell your home.

A transferable CertainTeed warranty is a marketing asset when you list your home. Buyers in the Austin and Round Rock market are sophisticated — they know a 10-year-old roof with a lifetime transferable warranty from a major manufacturer is a different situation than a 10-year-old roof with no documentation. It reduces negotiating leverage against you and can support a stronger asking price.

When we register your warranty with CertainTeed after installation, that documentation doesn't disappear. It follows the roof.
