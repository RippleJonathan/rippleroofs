## 1. Hail Damage (The #1 Pflugerville Problem)

### Why It's Common in Pflugerville

Pflugerville sits in 'Hail Alley'‚Äîthe area between Austin and Round Rock that gets hammered by severe storms each spring. Your roof is particularly vulnerable during March-May when warm, moist Gulf air collides with cold fronts.

**Recent storms that damaged Pflugerville roofs:**
- March 2024: Golf ball-sized hail in Blackhawk
- April 2023: Baseball-sized hail near Pflugerville Lake
- May 2022: Severe storms across Falcon Pointe and Celebration

### What Hail Damage Looks Like

**On asphalt shingles:**
- Dark spots (bruising where granules are knocked off)
- Exposed black mat underneath
- Cracked or dented shingles
- Loss of protective granules

**On other surfaces:**
- Dented gutters and downspouts
- Damaged roof vents and pipe boots
- Cracked skylights
- Dented AC unit (check the fins!)

### How to Fix It

**Immediate steps:**
1. Document everything with photos (inside and outside)
2. Call your insurance company within 48 hours
3. Get a professional inspection from a [certified contractor](/about)
4. Avoid door-to-door storm chasers (they disappear after taking deposits)

**Long-term solution:**
- Most hail damage requires full roof replacement
- Upgrade to impact-resistant shingles (Class 4 rating)
- Insurance typically covers 100% minus deductible
- Consider CertainTeed Landmark IR shingles (hail-resistant + great warranty)

**Cost:** $10,000-$18,000 for full replacement (usually covered by insurance)  
**Timeline:** 1-2 days for installation after insurance approval

**Prevention tip:** After major storms, have your roof inspected even if you don't see obvious damage. Hail damage accelerates deterioration.

## 2. Heat and UV Damage (Pflugerville's Summer Enemy)

### Why It's Common in Pflugerville

Pflugerville summers are brutal. With 100+ consecutive days over 95¬∞F and intense UV exposure, your roof essentially 'cooks' for months at a time.

**How heat damages roofs:**
- Asphalt shingles become brittle and crack
- Granules loosen and wash into gutters
- Sealant strips dry out and lift
- Ventilation issues cause attic temperatures to reach 150¬∞F+

### What Heat Damage Looks Like

- Curling or cupping shingle edges
- Excessive granule loss (check gutters after rain)
- Cracked or brittle shingles that break when touched
- Faded, discolored patches
- Blistering (bubbles under shingles)

### How to Fix It

**For minor damage:**
1. Replace individual damaged shingles ($150-$400)
2. Improve attic ventilation (ridge vents + soffit vents)
3. Add radiant barrier in attic to reduce heat transfer

**For extensive damage:**
- Full roof replacement with heat-resistant materials
- Choose light-colored shingles (reflect more heat)
- Consider metal roofing (extremely heat-resistant)

**Cost:** 
- Minor repairs: $150-$800
- Ventilation upgrades: $500-$1,500
- Full replacement: $10,000-$20,000

**Prevention tip:** Install proper attic ventilation. Every Pflugerville home should have adequate intake (soffit vents) and exhaust (ridge or box vents) to keep attic temps manageable.

## 3. Wind Damage (High Winds and Lifting Shingles)

### Why It's Common in Pflugerville

Pflugerville's open spaces and proximity to the Blackland Prairie mean fewer wind barriers. Spring and summer thunderstorms regularly produce 50-70 mph gusts that can rip shingles right off your roof.

### What Wind Damage Looks Like

- Missing shingles (entire pieces gone)
- Lifted or creased shingles (wind got underneath)
- Damaged flashing around chimneys and vents
- Torn or exposed underlayment
- Debris damage from flying tree branches

### How to Fix It

**Immediate action:**
1. Tarp any exposed areas to prevent water intrusion
2. Document damage for insurance
3. Remove any debris carefully (don't walk on damaged roof)
4. Call for emergency repairs

**Repair options:**
- Replace missing shingles and re-seal edges
- Install new flashing if compromised
- If widespread, full replacement may be more cost-effective

**Cost:**
- Emergency tarping: $300-$800
- Minor wind repairs: $500-$2,000
- Full replacement: $10,000-$20,000

**Prevention tip:** Have annual inspections to ensure shingles are properly sealed. Wind damage often starts with old, poorly sealed shingles that lift more easily.

## 4. Storm Water Pooling and Poor Drainage

### Why It's Common in Pflugerville

Pflugerville's flat-to-moderate terrain and clay-heavy soil mean drainage is critical. When heavy rains hit (and they do‚Äîremember the flash flooding?), water needs somewhere to go. If your roof has low-slope areas or clogged gutters, water pools.

**Why it matters:**
- Standing water accelerates shingle deterioration
- Leaks develop at seams and flashing
- Weight stress on roof structure
- Interior water damage and mold

### What Drainage Problems Look Like

- Water stains on ceilings after rain
- Sagging roof sections
- Clogged or overflowing gutters
- Mold or mildew smell in attic
- Moss or algae growth on roof

### How to Fix It

**Immediate fixes:**
1. Clean gutters and downspouts (do this quarterly)
2. Check for debris blocking drainage paths
3. Ensure downspouts direct water 6+ feet from foundation

**Long-term solutions:**
- Install gutter guards (reduces maintenance)
- Add additional downspouts if needed
- Improve roof slope in problem areas (requires professional)
- Add extra drainage for low spots

**Cost:**
- Gutter cleaning: $100-$200
- Gutter guards: $800-$2,000
- Drainage improvements: $500-$3,000
- Roof slope correction: $2,000-$8,000

**Prevention tip:** Clean gutters in spring and fall. Pflugerville's oak trees drop a lot of leaves that clog systems quickly.

## 5. Aging Roof Systems (Pflugerville's Growth Boom)

### Why It's Common in Pflugerville

Many Pflugerville neighborhoods were built during the 2000s-2010s growth boom. If your home is 15-20+ years old, your original roof is approaching (or past) its lifespan.

**Neighborhoods with aging roofs:**
- Falcon Pointe (built 2005-2010)
- Blackhawk (2000-2008)
- Brookfield (2005-2012)
- Celebration (2006-2014)

### What Aging Looks Like

- Widespread granule loss (shingles look 'bald')
- Curling, cupping, or brittleness
- Multiple leaks in different areas
- Daylight visible through roof boards in attic
- Sagging roof line

### How to Fix It

**Reality check:** If your roof is 20+ years old with multiple issues, replacement is more cost-effective than constant repairs.

**Replacement process:**
1. Get 2-3 estimates from [certified contractors](/contact)
2. Choose quality materials (don't cheap out‚Äîyou get 20-30 years from this)
3. Schedule during mild weather (spring or fall)
4. Plan for 1-3 day installation

**Material options for Pflugerville:**
- **Architectural shingles:** $10,000-$16,000 (25-30 year lifespan, great value)
- **Impact-resistant shingles:** $12,000-$18,000 (hail protection + insurance discounts)
- **Metal roofing:** $15,000-$30,000 (50+ years, energy-efficient, great for Texas heat)

**Prevention tip:** Don't wait for leaks. If your roof is 18+ years old, get an inspection. Proactive replacement prevents interior damage.

## Pflugerville-Specific Roofing Challenges

### Clay Soil and Foundation Movement

Pflugerville's expansive clay soil shifts with moisture changes. This can cause:
- Roof stress and cracking
- Flashing separation
- Structural movement affecting roof integrity

**Solution:** Ensure proper foundation drainage and watch for cracks or separation.

### Oak and Cedar Trees

Pflugerville's tree-lined neighborhoods are beautiful but create roofing issues:
- Falling branches during storms
- Leaves clogging gutters
- Shade promoting moss/algae growth

**Solution:** Trim branches 6+ feet from roof. Clean gutters regularly. Consider zinc strips for moss prevention.

### Rapid Temperature Swings

Pflugerville can experience 40¬∞F temperature changes in 24 hours. This expansion/contraction cycle stresses roofing materials.

**Solution:** Choose flexible, Texas-rated materials. Ensure proper attic ventilation to reduce thermal stress.
