## What Is Decra Roofing? The Premium Difference

Decra is manufactured by DECRA Roofing Systems, owned by Fletcher Building, a New Zealand-based global building products corporation. Decra invented stone-coated steel metal roofing in the 1950s to solve the aesthetic objection to bare metal roofing while delivering metal's 50+ year lifespan and superior performance.

### What Makes Decra Premium vs Competitors

**1. Thicker Steel Substrate**

**Decra:** 24-gauge steel (thickest in industry)  
**Competitors:** 26-gauge steel (Gerard, Metro Tiles, Boral)

**Why It Matters:**
- 24-gauge is 10-15% thicker than 26-gauge
- More dent-resistant (better hail performance)
- Stronger panels (better wind uplift resistance)
- Longer lifespan (more corrosion allowance over decades)

**Real-World Impact:** After severe Texas hailstorms, Decra roofs show fewer dents and virtually zero failures compared to thinner competitors.

**2. Superior Zincalume Coating**

Decra uses proprietary Zincalume (aluminum-zinc alloy) corrosion protection coating on steel substrate.

**Zincalume Advantages:**
- Better corrosion resistance than galvanized steel
- Handles Texas humidity and coastal salt air
- Self-healing properties (if scratched, coating spreads to protect)
- 50+ year corrosion protection in Texas climate

**3. Best-in-Industry Granule Adhesion**

The stone granule coating is what makes stone-coated steel look like traditional roofing. Cheap brands lose granules over time, exposing metal and degrading appearance.

**Decra Granule Performance:**
- Proprietary acrylic adhesive formula (industry secret)
- 95%+ granule retention after 30 years (best in industry)
- Natural basalt rock chips (fade-resistant, UV stable)
- Minimal granule loss even in severe Texas hail

**Comparison:**
- Decra: 95%+ granule retention at 30 years
- High-quality competitors (Gerard): 90-92% retention
- Budget stone-coated: 80-85% retention

**Why It Matters:** Granule loss exposes steel (looks worn), reduces heat reflection (higher cooling costs), and voids appearance warranty.
