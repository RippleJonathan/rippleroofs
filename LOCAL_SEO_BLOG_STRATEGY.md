## üí° Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Internal Linking
- Every blog post should link to 2-3 relevant service pages
- Link to your location pages when mentioning cities
- Create content hubs (e.g., "Complete Round Rock Roofing Resource Center")

### External Linking
- Link to credible sources (NOAA weather data, insurance company pages, CertainTeed)
- Builds trust and SEO authority
- May earn reciprocal links

- Include local photos (Round Rock landmarks, actual projects)
